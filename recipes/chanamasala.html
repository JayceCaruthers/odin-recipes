Chana Masala

Savory Indian chickpeas served over rice

Ingredients needed

  • 1 Onion, chopped
  • 1 Tomato, chopped
  • 1 (one inch) piece of fresh ginger, peeled and chopped
  • 4 cloves of garlic
  • 1 green chili pepper, seededn and chopped
  • 3 tablespoons of olive oil
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 1 tsp. each of chili powder, coriander powder and garam masala
  • 1/2 tsp. of turmeric powder
  • a pinch of salt
  • water (as needed)
  • 1 (15 oz.) can of chickpeas
  • Fresh cilantro leaves (for garnish)

Cooking instructions

  1. Grind the onion, tomato, ginger, garlic, and chile pepper together in a food processor until it forms a paste
  2. Heat the olive oil in a skillet over medium heat. Fy bay leaves in hot oil until the oil begins to separate and the mixture is golden brown (2-3 mins.) Season the mixture with the spices and salt.
  3. Stir enough water into the mixture to get a thick gravy; bring to a boil and stir chickpeas into the gravy. Reduce to medium heat and cook until the chickpeas are heated through (5-7 mins.) Garnish with cilantro.
